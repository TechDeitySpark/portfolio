About Me

From Dhaka to Chemnitz, my journey through technology has been driven by curiosity, adaptability, and a deep commitment to problem-solving. I began as a competitive programmer, participating in national contests across Bangladesh, where I honed my analytical and logical thinking early in my career. Over the past decade, I’ve evolved into a Full Stack Developer with a strong foundation in system design and scalable architecture. At Interstoff Apparels Ltd, I led the backend development of large-scale ERP systems tailored to manufacturing needs. Later, at Global Charge Ltd in London, I developed real-time payment solutions using Spring Boot and Node.js, integrating WebSocket and Docker for performance and deployment.

Now based in Germany and pursuing my Master’s in Web Engineering at TU Chemnitz, I’m expanding my skill set into cloud technologies like Kubernetes and AWS to architect resilient, future-ready systems. I’m passionate about building efficient, maintainable, and impactful solutions — whether it's for enterprises, startups, or open-source ecosystems. I’m currently seeking full-time or remote opportunities where I can contribute technical depth, system thinking, and cross-functional collaboration to high-impact teams.

My diverse experiences have equipped me with a unique blend of technical skills and creative problem-solving abilities, enabling me to adapt to various challenges in the tech landscape. My passion for technology extends beyond coding; I enjoy mentoring aspiring developers and sharing knowledge through workshops and online platforms. I believe in the power of community and collaboration in driving innovation and fostering growth.
